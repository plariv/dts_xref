 123          <h3>ABOUT THE RASCH MODEL</h3>
 124          <p>Dr. Solloway is using the <a href="http://www.rasch.org/">Rasch Model</a> to develop the SMS. First introduced over 50 years ago, the Rasch model, which is the most stringent of a family of models that are informed by item response theory, gradually has become the &quot;gold standard&quot; in scale development. </p>
 125          <p>If you are interested in learning more about this model, we recommend a visit to the site of the Institute for Objective Measurement (<a href="http://www.rasch.org/">IOM</a>). We also recommend the following chapters and articles:</p>
