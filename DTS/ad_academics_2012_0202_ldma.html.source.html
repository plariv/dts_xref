 210      <h1>THE LDMA 2.0 </h1>
 211      <p>Dealing constructively and creatively with complexity is one of the greatest challenges leaders face. Leadership experts, researchers, and the heads of industry and government all point to the need for leaders who can handle the increasing demands of our dynamic and crisis-riddled world. In response, thought leaders have called for leadership development initiatives focused on a set of 21st Century skills, including:</p>
 212      <ul>
 213        <li><em><strong>collaborative capacity:</strong></em> the ability to bring together diverse perspectives to develop innovative and effective solutions;</li>
 214        <li><em><strong>cognitive complexity:</strong></em> the ability to think well about complex, multifaceted, thorny issues; and</li>
 215        <li><em><strong>contextual thinking:</strong></em> the ability to consider problems in terms of the broader systems and contexts in which they are embedded.</li>
 216      </ul>
 217      <h3>Supporting leadership development and research</h3>
 218      <p>DTS supports leadership development with evidence-based, diagnostic, and educative assessments designed to optimize leadership development. The Lectical (Leadership) Decision Making Assessment (LDMA) and its reflective journaling companion (the LDMA Journal), represent a major methodological advance in the measurement of leadership development. Based on over a decade of research and practice, the LDMA measures eight vital leadership skills, all of which represent aspects of collaborative capacity, cognitive complexity, and contextual thinking. The LDMA is a:</p>
 219      <ul>
 220        <li><em><strong>complexity index</strong></em> that measures the developmental complexity of reasoning, </li>
 221        <li><em><strong>complexity catalyst</strong></em> that provides teachers and learners with richly educative feedback targeted to meet the individual learning needs of test takers, </li>
 222        <li><em><strong>research instrument</strong></em> that documents the effects of learning interventions in real time, and </li>
 223        <li>an <em><strong>educational technology</strong></em> that employs a sophisticated web-based platform to support leadership development.</li>
 224      </ul>
 225      <h3>Building knowledge</h3>
 226      <p>The rigorous research base and excellent psychometric properties of the LDMA provide a powerful set of tools for advancing leadership education and research. The Developmental Testing Service collaborated with leadership educators, researchers, and practitioners around the world for more than a decade to develop the LDMA. We are now ready to expand our network of collaboratorsâ€”supporting leaders, building knowledge about leader development, and advancing the research and practice that shape leadership education.</p>
