 441  <h1>ABOUT DTS</h1>
 442                <p>At the Developmental Testing Service, Inc. (DTS), we are dedicated to designing and delivering assessments that capture  major transformations in thinking that occur up to 14 times over the course of the lifespan. Each of these transformations leads to a more complex and integrated way of thinking. Whereas traditional tests do a good job examining <em>what</em> people know, our tests examine <em>the way people think about what they know</em>.</p>
 443  <p>We are further dedicated to transforming testing by changing the way people think about assessment. Most tests provide information about how well an individual is performing relative to others. Our tests   also tell learners (and their teachers, employers, clinicians or mentors) what they are most likley to benefit from learning next. This turns testing into what it should be—part of the <a href="http://theodawson.net/?p=204">learning process</a>. To accomplish these goals, we design reliable small- and large-scale assessments of intellectual development in a range of knowledge domains.            </p>
