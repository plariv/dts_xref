    <h1>INTRODUCING THE LDMA 2.0 </h1>
    <p> Leaders and managers in our dynamic and increasingly complex world face tremendous challenges that require 21st century leadership skills such as:</p>
    <ul>
      <li><em><strong>collaborative capacity:</strong></em> the ability to bring together diverse perspectives to develop innovative and effective solutions;</li>
      <li><em><strong>cognitive complexity:</strong></em> the ability to think well about complex, multifaceted,   thorny issues; and</li>
      
      <li><em><strong>contextual thinking:</strong></em> the ability to consider problems in terms of the broader systems and contexts in which they are embedded.  </li>
    </ul>
    <p>To keep up with constantly changing conditions, today's leaders and managers must continually develop their abilities. DTS provides evidence-based, diagnostic, and educative assessments designed to optimize development. The Lectical (Leadership) Decision Making Assessment (LDMA) targets <strong>eight vital leadership skills</strong>, all of which represent aspects of collaborative capacity, cognitive complexity, and contextual thinking, and provides <strong>rich educational feedback</strong> that profiles a test taker’s <strong>current and potential strengths</strong> and provides targeted<strong> learning recommendations</strong>. </p>
    <h3>Evidence-based assessment</h3>
    <p>The LDMA and its reflective journaling companion (the LDMA Journal) are based on more than a decade of rigorous research and practice involving leadership consultants, coaches, researchers, and educators from all over the world.</p>
    <p>The LDMA represents a major advance in the measurement of leadership development, and provides a powerful set of tools for optimizing leadership training and education. We are now ready to expand our network of clients working to support leaders’ learning and growth. </p>
    <p>We invite you to explore the LDMA in more depth on our website, and welcome your inquiries. </p>
