<h1>WHAT IS CLAS<sup>®</sup>?</h1>
	<p>CLAS is a computer assisted developmental scoring system that provides assessments of developmental level in a wide range of knowledge domains.  CLAS's algorithm was constructed by analyzing the grammatical structure of thousands of texts   scored with the Lectical Assessment System (<a href="../PDF/LAS.pdf">LAS</a>). Scoring with CLAS (originally called the <a href="../PDF/LAAS.pdf">LAAS</a>) has been shown to be as reliable as scoring with the LAS. </p>
		<p>Like the LAS, CLAS can be used:</p>
			<ul>
                  <li>to score Lectical Assessments;</li>
                  <li>to evaluate the effectiveness of interventions designed to promote cognitive development; </li>
                  <li>to track  development from year to year; </li>
                  <li>to trace conceptual development; </li>
                  <li>in comparative research on conceptual development; and </li>
                  <li>in research on developmental processes.</li>
			</ul>
		<p>CLAS has two advantages over the LAS:</p>
		<ol>
				<li>it is entirely objective, and</li>
                <li>it  dramatically reduces the cost of scoring very large numbers of assessments.</li>
            </ol>
